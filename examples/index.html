How to Play

  • • Move your paddle left and right using Arrow Keys, A/D Keys, or Mouse
  • • Click or press SPACE to start the game
  • • Break all the blocks containing your business card information
  • • Blocks will regenerate after 5 seconds - keep playing!
  • • Press START GAME button to restart anytime
